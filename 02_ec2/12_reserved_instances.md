# Reserved instances
- We need to provide commitment of usage for 1 or 3 year
- Recommended to use Savings plans
- We cannot have mix of compute like ec2, lambda and fargate
- We pay for capacity even if the instances are not used

# types
## standard RI 
- Fixed AZ, instance_type
## Convertible RI 
- Can change instance_type and AZ and region
